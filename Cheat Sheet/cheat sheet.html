Guide to HTML Semantics

Semantic

Description

Composition
header Introduces your website logo, navigation links, search bar
nav Designates a section for helping navigate a website links to an external website or a different section of the webpage
main Represent the dominant contenf of a document's body paragraph(s) directly related to the page's topic
footer A section at the bottom of a page typically meant to display related information copyright data, author's name, external links
section Represents a standalone section. Should always go under a heading. paragraphs or sub-paragraphs about the topic
article An independent piece of information comment, forum post, interactive widget
aside Indirectly related content fun-facts
figure Self-contained content supporting the topic being discussed photograph, illustration, diagram
figcaption Short description of a figure caption, legend
video Embeds a media player supporting video playback clips, movies, documentaries
embed Embeds external content link
audio Sound content song, recording
